Spider-Man: Across the Spider-Verse

After reuniting with Gwen Stacy, Brooklyn's full-time, friendly
neighborhood Spider-Man is catapulted across the Multiverse,
where he encounters a team of Spider-People charged with protecting
its very existence. However, when the heroes clash on how to handle
a new threat, Miles finds himself pitted against the other Spiders.
He must soon redefine what it means to be a hero,
so he can save the people he loves most.
